"Journey Through Cytoplasm" is a video game developed by Samih EL SAKHAWI. This application is an arcade game, where you play a kinesin in the cytoplasm of a cell, and you have to go as far as possible.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding the policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information if anyone decided to use this game.

If you choose to use this game, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy. Collected data

"Journey Through Cytoplasm" does not collect identifiable informations like name, email, phone number, etc. However, "Journey Through Cytoplasm" displays ads. For that purpose, location datas are collected for targeted advertising.


Requested permissions

Depending on your device and OS version, "Journey Through Cytoplasm" may request you to approve certain permissions. Below is a list of these permissions and informations on why the game asks for these permissions.


Android.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION:

"Journey Through Cytoplasm" needs access to your device's location data for targeted advertising.


Android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE:

Allows "Journey Through Cytoplasm" to access information about networks.


Android.permission.INTERNET:

"Journey Through Cytoplasm" needs access to internet so that it can, amongst other things:
